About

I am a software developer currently based in Calgary, Canada. My passion for programming started back in 2014 when I was trying to make a website to make a quick buck. I had no idea what I was doing, but after many tries I finally installed WordPress on a live domain and begun publishing. Couple months later I figured websites were built in HTML, so I started learning the language, mostly from a printed 40ish pages book. It was so much fun, I figured I had learned a lot about web design and development, and continued to learn CSS, PHP then JavaScript.

Fast forward to two years later, I had developed so many skills and accumulated knowledge mostly from the free internet, I decided to start pitching for gigs and I entered the world of freelance.

But just before that, I had built a dozen of plugins for WordPress platform, and sold copies on Envato marketplace, but it was a bit of a burden providing product support and working on features simultaneously, I've decided to sell my business and shift to freelancing.

During my years of freelancing I've had the pleasure to work with a handful of clients from all over the world and expanded my skillset. Heck, I even traveled abroad for the first time and managed to get stuck in the Philippines for 6 months, all while working remotely. Moving forward I've decided to get a degree in this field and I am currently at the initial phase of doing so with Bow Valley College.
